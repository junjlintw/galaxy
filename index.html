Galaxy Program

The Galaxy Program admits students who may not be the best, but show strong potential to succeed in academia. After admission, each student must choose a unique celestial body in the Milky Way, such as a star, planet, or natural satellite, as their code name. Admission is both an honor and a responsibility. Students are expected to support each other and carry out serious academic work. The main goal of the Galaxy Program is to help each member find its position in the vast academic universe, no matter where it is.

If you are interested in joining, please send an email to junjielin@arizona.edu with your CV and a one-page research interest statement attached. The application fee is $150. Applications will only be reviewed after payment is confirmed. No notifications or refunds will be provided for unsuccessful applications. If admitted, you will be required to pay an additional $350 and obtain at least two recommendation letters from active members to become a full member. Membership benefits include mentorship opportunities, a quarterly newsletter, professional networking events, Christmas postcards, and one free annual Chinese restaurant gathering. Please note that this is a highly exclusive program, and we prefer students with strong potential to succeed in academia. If your application is unsuccessful, do not be discouraged. We believe there is a place for you somewhere in the galaxy, just not in this one.
