Pluto

Pluto (Jun-Jie Lin) is a doctoral candidate in Sociology at the University of Arizona. Under the supervision of Dr. Diego Leal, his dissertation examines network dynamics across the life course, focusing on the causes and impacts of social tie changes and their consequences for social inequality. Beyond social network analysis, his research interests extend to multiple forms of spatial inequality. He earned his Master’s degree in Sociology from National Taipei University. Junjie employs a mixed-methods approach in his scholarship, utilizing in-depth interviews, hierarchical linear models, and stochastic actor-oriented models SAOMs.

Outside academia, he is highly enthusiastic about baseball. He also enjoys reading novels and engaging in literary writing.
